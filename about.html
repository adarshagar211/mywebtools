About WebTools

WebTools is a free, privacy-friendly collection of developer utilities built for speed, simplicity, and accuracy. Whether you need to format JSON, encode text, convert data, or generate quick hashes, our tools run entirely in your browser — no tracking, no server-side storage.

Fast & Secure

All tools are optimized for instant results using pure client-side JavaScript. HTTPS and modern Content Security Policies keep your data safe while you work.

Modular & Growing

We’re constantly adding new encoders, formatters, and converters based on developer feedback. Have a suggestion? Send it via our contact form below!
